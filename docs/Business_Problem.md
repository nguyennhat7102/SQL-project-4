# Business Problem

## Background

AdventureWorks is a bicycle and accessories retailer operating across multiple international markets. Although the company has experienced significant revenue growth over recent years, management lacks a comprehensive understanding of the key drivers behind revenue performance, product profitability, and customer retention.

To support data-driven decision-making, the company seeks to leverage historical sales data to identify growth opportunities, optimize profitability, and better understand customer purchasing behavior.

---

# Business Objectives

This project aims to address four key business objectives.

## 1. Understand Revenue Growth Drivers

Management wants to answer the following questions:

- How has revenue evolved over time?
- Which markets contribute the most revenue?
- Are there seasonal sales patterns?
- Is the business overly dependent on a small number of products or territories?

### Business Value

The findings will help the company:

- Allocate resources more effectively.
- Prioritize high-performing markets.
- Reduce revenue concentration risk.

---

## 2. Optimize Product Profitability

High revenue does not necessarily translate into high profitability.

Management wants to understand:

- Which product categories generate the highest profit?
- Which products generate strong sales but weak margins?
- Are there opportunities to improve profitability through product mix optimization?

### Business Value

The findings will help the company:

- Focus investment on high-margin product categories.
- Improve product portfolio decisions.
- Increase profitability without relying solely on revenue growth.

---

## 3. Understand Customer Purchasing Behavior

The company wants to gain deeper insights into customer activity by answering:

- Do customers return for additional purchases?
- How long does it typically take customers to repurchase?
- How effectively are customers retained over time?

### Business Value

The findings will help the company:

- Improve customer retention.
- Design more effective loyalty and engagement programs.
- Increase Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

---

## 4. Identify Future Growth Opportunities

Management also wants to determine:

- Which customer groups contribute the largest share of revenue?
- Which product categories should be expanded?
- Which territories offer the greatest growth potential?

### Business Value

The findings will help the company:

- Develop long-term growth strategies.
- Improve resource allocation.
- Support strategic planning and investment decisions.

---

# Analytical Approach

To address these objectives, the project applies a combination of sales, profitability, customer, and market analyses.

### Revenue Analysis

- Revenue Trend Analysis
- Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth Analysis
- Seasonality Analysis

### Product Analysis

- Product Performance Analysis
- Pareto Analysis (80/20 Rule)
- Profit Pool Analysis
- Revenue-Margin Segmentation

### Customer Analysis

- Repeat Purchase Analysis
- Repurchase Cycle Analysis
- Cohort Retention Analysis

### Market Analysis

- Revenue by Territory Analysis
- Territory Contribution Analysis

---

# Expected Outcomes

By the end of this project, AdventureWorks will be able to:

- Identify the primary drivers of revenue growth.
- Understand which products and categories generate the highest profitability.
- Measure customer retention and purchasing behavior.
- Discover opportunities to increase profit and customer lifetime value.
- Make more informed business decisions based on data rather than intuition.
